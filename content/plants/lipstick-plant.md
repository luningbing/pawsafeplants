Aeschynanthus radicans is a trailing epiphyte native to Southeast Asia. It’s a popular hanging basket plant due to its arching stems and vibrant blooms. The plant prefers consistently moist soil and benefits from regular misting. With proper care, it can flower profusely throughout the growing season, adding a splash of color to any room.
