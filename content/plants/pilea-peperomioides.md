For cat owners, this plant is a dream come true, as it is completely non-toxic to both felines and canines, allowing pets to coexist safely with this botanical gem. Easy to care for, the Pilea thrives in bright, indirect light and signals its watering needs by slightly drooping its leaves. Its unique form and pet-friendly status make it an ideal choice for adding a touch of playful sophistication to any shelf or tabletop."
