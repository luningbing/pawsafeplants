---
title: "Lilies"
scientific_name: "Lilium spp."
toxicity_level: "⚠️ EXTREME DANGER"
summary: "All parts of lilies are highly toxic to cats—even small ingestions can cause kidney failure."
ascpa_link: "https://www.aspca.org/pet-care/animal-poison-control/toxic-and-non-toxic-plants/lily"
symptoms:
  - Vomiting
  - Lethargy
  - Loss of appetite
  - Kidney failure (within 36–72 hours)
what_to_do: |
  **Contact your vet or ASPCA Animal Poison Control IMMEDIATELY.**
  Do not wait for symptoms. Early treatment is critical.
