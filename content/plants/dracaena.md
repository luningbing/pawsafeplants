Dracaena marginata is native to Madagascar and thrives in bright, indirect light but adapts to lower light conditions. It is drought-tolerant and susceptible to root rot if overwatered. The plant can grow quite tall indoors with proper care. Its toxicity is due to saponins, which can cause gastrointestinal upset in cats.
