---
title: "ZZ Plant"
scientific_name: "Zamioculcas zamiifolia"
common_names:
  - "Zanzibar Gem"
  - "Eternity Plant"
toxicity_level: "⚠️ MODERATE TOXICITY – ORAL IRRITATION"
summary: "Popular easy-care indoor plant containing calcium oxalate crystals that can cause oral irritation and GI upset if ingested by cats."
ascpa_link: "https://www.aspca.org/pet-care/animal-poison-control/toxic-and-non-toxic-plants/zz-plant"
image: "/images/zz-plant.jpg"
symptoms:
  - "Oral pain and swelling"
  - "Drooling"
  - "Vomiting"
  - "Decreased appetite"
what_to_do: |
  **Prompt action is key to minimize discomfort.**

  1. Rinse your cat’s mouth with clean water.
  2. Offer a small amount of milk or yogurt to soothe burning.
  3. Contact your veterinarian for advice.
  4. Monitor for any worsening symptoms.

safe_alternatives:
  - "Spider Plant (Chlorophytum comosum)"
  - "Bamboo Palm (Chamaedorea seifrizii)"
  - "Boston Fern (Nephrolepis exaltata)"
