---
title: Potato Plant
scientific_name: Solanum tuberosum
toxicity_level: ⚠️ MILD TO MODERATE TOXICITY – ORAL AND GASTROINTESTINAL IRRITATION
summary: >-
  Potato plants contain solanine which can cause gastrointestinal upset and
  neurological symptoms in cats.
image: >-
  https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1577173175952-d4c018c6915f?ixlib=rb-4.0.3&w=800&h=600&fit=crop&auto=format
care_difficulty: Medium
common_names:
  - Potato Plant
  - Potato
is_flower: false
ascpa_link: >-
  https://www.aspca.org/pet-care/animal-poison-control/toxic-and-non-toxic-plants/potato
symptoms:
  - Vomiting
  - Diarrhea
  - Lethargy
  - Weakness
what_to_do: >-
  **If your cat ingests potato plant:**


  1. Remove any remaining plant material from your cat's mouth.

  2. Rinse the mouth thoroughly with cool water.

  3. Offer a small amount of water or milk to help flush the system.

  4. Monitor for symptoms and contact your veterinarian if they persist or
  worsen.

  5. Do not induce vomiting unless specifically instructed by your vet.


  **Prevention:**

  - Keep Potato Plant out of reach of curious cats

  - Consider placing in hanging baskets or high shelves

  - Provide cat-safe alternatives like cat grass or catnip
safe_alternatives:
  - Sweet Potato Vine
  - Catnip
