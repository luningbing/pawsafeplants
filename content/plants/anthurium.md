Anthurium andraeanum, native to Colombia and Ecuador, thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. Regular misting and consistent moisture keep it healthy. The plant’s waxy spathes can last for weeks, making it a popular gift. Keep away from pets, as ingestion leads to swelling of the mouth, vomiting, and difficulty swallowing.
