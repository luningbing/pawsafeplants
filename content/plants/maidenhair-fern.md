Adiantum raddianum originates from tropical regions and thrives in high humidity and consistently moist soil. It dislikes dry air and prefers indirect light. While it can be a bit finicky about moisture, its graceful appearance is worth the care. It’s an excellent choice for bathrooms or well-humidified rooms.
