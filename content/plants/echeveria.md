Echeveria elegans is native to Mexico and thrives in bright light and well-draining soil. It requires infrequent watering, making it ideal for busy owners. The plant stores water in its plump leaves, which often have a delicate powdery coating. In summer, it may produce pinkish-yellow flowers on tall stalks.
