  outcomes are rare.

what_to_do: |-
  **If your cat consumes geranium, contact your vet.**

  1. Rinse the mouth gently with water.
  2. Offer a small amount of milk or water to dilute irritants.
  3. Keep your cat hydrated and monitor for worsening symptoms.
  4. Seek veterinary care if vomiting or diarrhea continues.
