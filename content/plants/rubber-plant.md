Ficus elastica, native to Southeast Asia, is a robust houseplant that can grow quite tall. It tolerates a range of light conditions but prefers bright, indirect light. Water when the top inch of soil is dry. The plant's milky sap can cause skin irritation, and ingestion by cats leads to symptoms due to calcium oxalate crystals.
