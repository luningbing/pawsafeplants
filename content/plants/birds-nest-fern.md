For pet owners, this fern is a true gem, as it is completely non-toxic to both cats and dogs, ensuring a safe and serene environment for all household members. While it thrives in high humidity and indirect light, its striking form and lush appearance make the extra care worthwhile. The Bird's Nest Fern is an excellent choice for adding a touch of sophisticated greenery and natural artistry to your home, without any safety concerns for your curious pets."
