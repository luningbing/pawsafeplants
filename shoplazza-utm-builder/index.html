How to build a Shoplazza UTM link

  1. Paste the final Shoplazza product, collection, or landing-page URL into the free builder.
  2. Choose the traffic channel and keep the source and medium names consistent.
  3. Name the campaign by promotion or launch, not by a vague label like test.
  4. Add utm_content only when you need creative, ad, or placement detail.
  5. Click the final URL and verify the first visit in GA4 Realtime.
https://example.com/products/sample-product?utm_source=meta&utm_medium=paid_social&utm_campaign=summer_launch&utm_content=ugc_video_02

Good field meanings

Parameter Good example Meaning
utm_source meta The ad platform, creator platform, email tool, or partner.
utm_medium paid_social The traffic category you want to compare.
utm_campaign summer_launch The campaign, promo, launch, or sales push.
utm_content ugc_video_02 The creative, ad, creator, placement, or asset.

Shoplazza checks before launch

  • Check the exact final URL after redirects, not only the URL you copied.
  • Do not mix old UTMs already on the product URL with new generated UTMs.
  • Verify source, medium, campaign, and content in GA4 Realtime.
  • Keep personal customer data and order data out of URL parameters.
