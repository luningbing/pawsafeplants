Beginner-safe path

Do not know UTM or GA4 yet? Start with one link.

Paste the real landing page URL from your store, choose one traffic channel, keep beginner mode on, and leave optional fields blank if you are not sure. The goal is a clean ad URL you can click and check before spending.

  1. Paste the ad landing page Use the exact product, collection, or landing page your ad will send shoppers to.
  2. Pick one channel Choose Meta, Google, TikTok, affiliate, email, SMS, or the closest source you plan to launch first.
  3. Use a readable campaign name Plain names like spring_sale_bag are easier for small teams than advanced macros.
  4. Click and check GA4 Open the generated URL yourself, then use Realtime to see whether the visit appears.
