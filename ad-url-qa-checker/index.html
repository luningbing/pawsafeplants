      What this checker can catch

      • Missing utm_source, utm_medium, or utm_campaign.
      • Repeated UTM keys that can confuse reporting.
      • Private customer data such as email, phone, name, address, or order fields inside URL parameters.
      • Non-HTTPS URLs or values that should be reviewed before launch.

      What it cannot confirm

      This page does not crawl the destination, follow redirects, inspect checkout, or read your GA4 account. After this static check, click the final URL yourself and confirm the visit in GA4 Realtime.
