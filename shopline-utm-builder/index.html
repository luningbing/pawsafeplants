How to build a SHOPLINE UTM link

  1. Paste the final SHOPLINE product, collection, or campaign page URL into the homepage builder.
  2. Select the traffic channel, such as Meta, Google Ads, TikTok, affiliate, email, or SMS.
  3. Use a readable campaign name like spring_sale_best_sellers.
  4. Use utm_content for creative or placement detail only when you need it.
  5. Click the generated URL and verify the query string survives the landing page load.
https://example.com/products/sample-product?utm_source=tiktok&utm_medium=paid_social&utm_campaign=spring_sale_best_sellers&utm_content=creator_video_01

Recommended naming pattern

Field Example Use it for
utm_source tiktok The traffic platform or partner.
utm_medium paid_social The traffic type.
utm_campaign spring_sale_best_sellers The promotion, launch, or campaign.
utm_content creator_video_01 Creative, ad, creator, or placement detail.

SHOPLINE QA checks

  • Check whether the store redirects from one locale, currency, or path to another.
  • Make sure campaign parameters remain after any automatic redirect.
  • Verify GA4 Realtime before judging purchase attribution.
  • Keep private customer data out of URL parameters.
