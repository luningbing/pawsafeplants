How to use the batch output

  1. Generate the CSV and open at least one final URL yourself.
  2. Confirm the landing page opens without an unexpected redirect.
  3. Open GA4 Realtime and confirm the visit appears before spending.
  4. Use the CSV as your ad setup brief or spreadsheet source.

Safe limits

  • This tool does not shorten links or hide the destination.
  • It does not store your pasted URLs or send them to GA4.
  • It does not promise perfect attribution. Browser privacy, consent settings, redirects, checkout behavior, cross-domain flows, and blockers can still affect analytics.
